About us

Locals helping locals win online

Orvexa Media Strategies is a digital marketing growth agency based in Dallas, Texas, helping local businesses build their presence in an increasingly digital marketplace.

Our mission

To help small local businesses grow their online presence through consistent, high-quality social media content, at a price that makes sense for them.

Our vision

To become the go-to social media consultants in the DFW area, enabling local businesses to compete online and grow their digital presence.

Why this matters to us

This work is personal

The DFW area is one of the fastest-growing regions in the country — home to a rich, diverse community of entrepreneurs, family-owned businesses, and local service providers. These businesses are the restaurants, salons, gyms, and shops that define where we live. When they struggle, our community struggles with them.

Growing up here, we've watched talented, hardworking owners lose customers simply because they're invisible online. The problem isn't the quality of their product or service — it's the absence of a platform. That feels deeply unjust, and it's a gap we believe young, digitally-native entrepreneurs are uniquely positioned to fill.

We believe small businesses are the backbone of our local economies, and that they deserve the same opportunity to succeed online as anyone else. Helping them improve their social media means helping them reach more customers, increase revenue, and keep serving the community for years to come. When local businesses win, the whole community benefits.
